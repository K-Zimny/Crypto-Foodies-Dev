About

Crypto Foodies is a decentralized restaurant group creating the largest restaurant group in history. Our Web3 platform allows you to either build a brand, host a brand, or stake a brand utilizing the $CF token. Whether you are in the analog world or cruising the metaverse, when you see Crytpo Foodies the fuel you need to keep exploring.

How does it work

Think of the $CFood token as a virtual brand ETF; instead of investing in one particular brand, you are investing in the constellation of brands. The protocol is split between two entities, the Crypto Foodie Foundation and Company. The Foundation is community-driven and is tasked to oversee the development of the virtual restaurant ecosystem, provide meaningful interactions between creators and host restaurants, and create the rules, rewards, and responsibilities of the $CFood token. The Company receives Virtual Restaurants (VRs) from the foundation to package them into NFTs for lease on its trading platform. Once the leaseholder of the NFT receives the brand, they either, launch the brand in their physical location via 3rd party delivery apps or launch the brand in the metaverse with fulfillment coming from Host Restaurants (HR) in their area.
